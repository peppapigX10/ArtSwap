# Art Swap Community Website

## About
Heya! I'm Peperi, an artist, a writer, a designer, and a teen. 
I’m passionate about an idea that would bring community and bring joy to people’s
lives: an art swap! Everyone from kids to the elderly is invited to create a small, 
hand-sized art piece, clip it to a clothesline gallery stand (that I will maintain and create)
and swap it for another artist’s mini creation to keep!

Visit the website: [link](https://peppapigx10.github.io/ArtSwap/)

## What it Does
Talks about this art swap.
